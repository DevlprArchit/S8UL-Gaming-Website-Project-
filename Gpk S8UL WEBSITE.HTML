Soul Esports

Soul Esports, commonly known as Team Soul, is one of the most prominent and successful esports organizations in India. They have gained widespread recognition in the gaming community, particularly for their achievements in the mobile game "PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds Mobile" (PUBG Mobile). Soul Esports was founded by Naman "Mortal" Sandeep Mathur, who is a renowned PUBG Mobile player and content creator. The organization has been home to some of the best PUBG Mobile players Aswell As well knowned Gaming Content Creators in India. Team Soul has participated in various national and international PUBG Mobile tournaments, earning several titles and accolades. They have a massive fan following and have played a significant role in popularizing competitive mobile gaming in India.



Animesh Agarwal Aka 8bit-Thug

Naman Mathur Aka Soul Mortal

Soul Mortal whose real name is Naman Mathur, is an Indian professional esports player and content creator. He gained significant popularity as a competitive PUBG Mobile player and became one of the most well-known figures in the Indian gaming community. Soul Mortal was a member of Team Soul, a successful PUBG Mobile team based in India. The team achieved several accomplishments and won various tournaments in the Indian gaming circuit. Naman Mathur himself was praised for his exceptional skills and gameplay. Apart from his competitive career, Soul Mortal also created content on YouTube and other social media platforms. He was widely recognized for his entertaining gameplay videos, tutorials, and vlogs related to gaming and his personal life.
